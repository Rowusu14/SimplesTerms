About

Have you ever encountered a passage or document that was too long or cluttered to understand? SimplesTerms aims to solve this problem and keep you informed. Simply upload your PDF or TXT document of choice and recieve summarized results in seconds!

Our project began last year as a proposition that became the 1st Place winner and Crowd Choice pick of the Villanova Idea Challenge of 2023. SimplesTerms is a React program with Bootstrap customization {put new things we learned to make this here}
